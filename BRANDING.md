# 3PL Links Branding Guide

## Brand Overview
**Company:** 3PL Links  
**Tagline:** "Regain supply chain control. Find your way back to better profit."  
**Industry:** Third-Party Logistics, Supply Chain Management  
**Location:** Toronto, Ontario (25+ years operation)

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Navy Blue** `#0A2463` - Primary brand color (trust, logistics, reliability)
- **Deep Blue** `#1E3A8A` - Secondary brand color
- **Sky Blue** `#3B82F6` - Accent/interactive elements

### Secondary Colors  
- **Steel Gray** `#64748B` - Text, borders
- **Light Gray** `#F1F5F9` - Backgrounds, cards
- **White** `#FFFFFF` - Primary background

### Accent Colors
- **Success Green** `#059669` - Savings, positive metrics
- **Warning Orange** `#F59E0B` - Alerts, processing states
- **Error Red** `#DC2626` - Errors, critical issues
- **Info Blue** `#0EA5E9` - Information, neutral states

## Typography

### Font Stack
```css
font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', 'Roboto', 
  'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;
```

### Hierarchy
- **H1 (Hero):** 3rem (48px), Bold 700, -0.02em letter-spacing
- **H2 (Section):** 1.875rem (30px), Bold 700
- **H3 (Subsection):** 1.5rem (24px), Semibold 600
- **Body:** 1rem (16px), Regular 400
- **Small:** 0.875rem (14px), Regular 400
- **Label:** 0.75rem (12px), Medium 500, uppercase, 0.05em letter-spacing

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Company name: "3PL Links"
- Tagline: "Freight Audit Platform"
- Color: Navy Blue on light backgrounds, White on dark backgrounds

### Icon/Favicon
- Simple "3PL" monogram or link chain icon
- Navy Blue background with white text

## UI Components

### Cards
- Background: White
- Border: 1px solid #E2E8F0
- Border Radius: 12px
- Shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Hover: 0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.12), translateY(-4px)

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Navy Blue background, white text, 8px radius
- **Secondary:** White background, Navy Blue border & text
- **Success:** Success Green background, white text
- **Padding:** 0.875rem 2rem (14px 32px)
- **Hover:** Slight darkening, shadow lift

### Metrics
- **Positive (Savings):** Success Green
- **Neutral:** Navy Blue
- **Warning:** Warning Orange
- **Negative:** Error Red

## Design Principles

1. **Professional & Clean** - Logistics industry demands clarity
2. **Data-First** - Metrics and charts front and center
3. **Trust & Reliability** - Conservative color palette, clear hierarchy
4. **Efficiency** - Minimal clicks, clear CTAs
5. **Responsive** - Mobile-first, works on all devices

## Inspiration
- Industry: FedEx, UPS, DHL (professional logistics branding)
- Aesthetic: Clean, corporate, data-driven
- Audience: CEOs, CFOs, Operations Directors (executive-level users)
